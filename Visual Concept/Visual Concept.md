The other limitation: JavaFX. This is really dumb. I think I chose the wrong tools... but why not? It's an experiment. Make a game with JavaFX and GIMP. So... JavaFX lets you animate simple meshes. Alright! I'm gonna create a **world of billboards**! Beautifully rotated around three axis, illuminated a la Phong, and even animated! JavaFX let's you do that.



The best part: For simplicity the geometry of the meshes should always be just rectangles. 4 vertices. Ok. Other shapes, like circles, triangles or zig-zags, we simply paint on the texture. Where we don't want any matter we just don't paint on the texture, aka transparency.



The overall visual concept can be seen in the file 'visual\_concept.png'. See the green background? Green is a color that makes you feel good. The cards themselves are black and white... actually antique white... nice. Why? Well... There are colorblind people and this way the game remains fair. Also i sort of like the way it looks. Let's focus on the shapes exclusively! Let's see how this will work out.
